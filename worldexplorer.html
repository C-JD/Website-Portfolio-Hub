Cultural Celebrations

March

🌸 Holi Festival (India)

The Festival of Colors celebrates the arrival of spring and the victory of good over evil.

🎨 Colors represent joy and love 💃 Traditional dances and music 🍬 Special sweets and treats
May

🎌 Children's Day (Japan)

Kodomo no Hi celebrates children's happiness and honors their personalities.

🎏 Koinobori carp streamers ⚔️ Samurai armor displays 🍃 Kashiwa mochi treats
September

🌙 Mid-Autumn Festival (China)

A harvest festival celebrating family reunion and the full moon.

🥮 Mooncakes sharing 🏮 Lantern displays 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family gatherings
November

💀 Día de los Muertos (Mexico)

Day of the Dead honors deceased loved ones with vibrant celebrations.

🌺 Marigold decorations 🍞 Pan de muerto bread 🕯️ Altar offerings
