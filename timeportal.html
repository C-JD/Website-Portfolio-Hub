Historical Artifacts

🗿

Rosetta Stone

Ancient Egypt - 196 BCE

The key to deciphering Egyptian hieroglyphs, discovered in 1799. This granodiorite stele contains the same text in three scripts: hieroglyphic, demotic, and ancient Greek.

Material: Granodiorite
Location: British Museum
Significance: Deciphered hieroglyphs
